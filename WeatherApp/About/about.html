About My Weather Website

My weather website is designed to provide users with real-time weather information for cities around the world. I use data from the Weather by API-Ninjas API to ensure that the information displayed on my website is accurate and up-to-date.

Features
  • Real-time weather information for cities around the world
  • Current conditions and forecasted weather for the next few days
  • Temperature, humidity, wind speed, and other information

Troubleshooting

If you're experiencing issues with my website, please check the following:

  • Ensure that your internet connection is stable
  • Clear your browser's cache and cookies
  • Try searching for a different city
  • If the problem persists, please contact me for assistance, by clicking here Send Feedback.
